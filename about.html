Transitioning from a background in architectural engineering (bachelor degree) to the dynamic realm of User Experience (UX) Design, I've discovered my true passion lies in crafting solutions that not only enhance functionality but also enrich user experiences.

With a foundation in visual communication (master degree) and a thirst for knowledge, I've embarked on a journey of continuous learning, honing my skills through hands-on projects and professional courses. My diverse background fuels my creativity and ability to think critically, while my love for art & technology, and connecting with others drives my dedication to creating impactful designs.

From user research to interaction design, I thrive on the challenge of innovating solutions that push boundaries and make a tangible difference in people's lives. Product design isn't just a career for me; it's a calling to effect positive change, one interface at a time.
